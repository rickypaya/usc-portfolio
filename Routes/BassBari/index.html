Biography

Miami-native Michael Leyte-Vidal is a dynamic and versatile vocalist whose artistry effortlessly bridges genres, from the grandeur of opera to the electric energy of Broadway. A classically trained Bass/Baritone with a flair for riffs, runs, and improvisation, Michael brings both technical precision and charismatic stage presence to every performance. His formal training allowed him to hone his craft through an operatic residency. Now, with his sights set on Broadway, Michael combines his rich vocal prowess, theatrical expertise, and infectious charm to captivate audiences.

Beyond the stage, he shares his passion as a vocal coach, offering guidance to aspiring singers. Explore his journey through past performances—and discover a voice that resonates with power, versatility, and undeniable artistry.
